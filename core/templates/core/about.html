Our Mission

"We believe that response time is the ultimate metric of institutional safety. RapidResQ eliminates the gap between facility management and official first responders."

How It Started

In high-density zones like hospitality venues, schools, and corporate parks, traditional 911 systems introduce an average of 4-7 minutes of critical dispatch latency. Internal security often operates in silos, unaware of external first responder movements.

RapidResQ was born out of the Google Solution Challenge 2026. By engineering a low-latency socket mesh powered by modern language models, we designed a decentralized framework that provides immediate, localized alerts while simultaneously triaging data directly to the police, hospital, and fire department dashboards.
