Safety Drills & Training

Familiarize yourself with the institutional crisis standards before triggering structural alarms.

Launch Mock Drill (Simulated SOS)

Fire Outbreak

  1. Activate nearest pull station.
  2. Open RapidResQ app & press SOS.
  3. Select "Fire" from the voice prompt.
  4. Follow the dynamic green evacuation path shown on the Tactical Map.
  5. Do not use elevators.

Medical Emergency

  1. Ensure area safety first.
  2. Hold SOS for 3 seconds.
  3. Speak clearly into the AI voice prompt detailing the patient condition.
  4. Wait for Haversine logic to dispatch the closest internal medical staff.
  5. Administer basic first aid if trained.

Security Threat

  1. Avoid confronting the threat.
  2. Trigger Silent SOS (Triple Tap).
  3. The AI will listen to ambient noise to verify threat escalation.
  4. Barricade your location using the app's 'Lockdown' mode.
  5. Await Police clearance via Push Notification.
