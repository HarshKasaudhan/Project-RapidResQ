Fire Protocol

If your clothes catch fire

  1. 1. STOP: Do not run immediately.
  2. 2. DROP: Fall to the ground.
  3. 3. ROLL: Cover face, roll back & forth.

Medical Emergency

Basic CPR steps (Adult)

  • Call 911 or local emergency immediately.
  • Lay person flat on their back.
  • Place hands firmly on the center of chest.
  • Push hard & fast (100-120 beats/min).
  • Continue until help or an AED arrives.

Security Threat

Active Threat Protocol

RUN 1st Option

Evacuate the area if there is an accessible escape path. Leave belongings behind.

HIDE 2nd Option

If evacuation is impossible, find a place to hide. Block entry and silence your phone.

FIGHT Last Resort

Commit to aggressive physical action to incapacitate the threat.
